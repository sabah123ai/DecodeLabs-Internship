Plant Care Tips

Learn how to take care of your plants

Water Regularly

Water your plants regularly to keep them healthy.

Sunlight

Place your plants in a location where they can receive adequate sunlight.

Use Fertilizer

Feed your plants with fertilizer every few weeks during the growing season to provide essential nutrients.

Change pot

Change the pot of your plants annually to provide them with fresh soil.

Temperature

Keep your plants in a location with a stable temperature.

Prunning

Remove dead or yellow leaves to encourage healthy growth and keep the plant looking beautiful.

Overcrowd Plants

Don't overcrowd your plants; ensure they have enough space to grow.

Dry Leaves

Clean up dry or damaged leaves regularly to encourage new growth.
